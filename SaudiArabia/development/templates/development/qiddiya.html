Facts about this initiative:

• Qiddiya is located southwest of Riyadh on a site that spans 376 square kilometers.

• It is being developed by the Qiddiya Investment Company (QIC), which was incorporated in 2018 and is wholly owned by PIF.

• The giga-project is creating a cultural, sports and entertainment destination to improve the quality of life for Saudi Arabia’s residents and elevate its capital to become one of the world’s top 10 city economies​.

• The Six Flags Qiddiya theme park will be home to Falcon’s Flight – the world’s longest, tallest and fastest coaster.

• In 2022, Qiddiya awarded an SAR 2.8 billion contract to develop the region’s biggest water park and the first within Saudi Arabia.
